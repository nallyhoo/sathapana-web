Savings Account

Start preparing for your future with a Sathapana Bank Savings Account. Aside from earning a daily interest rate, which can be paid out monthly, the Sathapana Bank Savings Account provides a very convenient way to manage and access your money.

Available in Khmer Riel, US Dollar, or Thai Baht.

Deposit and withdraw funds anytime from our extensive network of ATMs throughout Cambodia. With a minimum of USD10 (KHR40, 000) deposit to open a savings account, you are on your way to a secured financial future.

Features & Benefits

USD KHR
Minimum Opening Balance USD 10 KHR 40,000
Minimum Ongoing Balance USD 5 KHR 20,000
Interest Rate 0.25% p.a. 0.75% p.a.
Interest Payment Daily accrued interest and Semi-annual paid
